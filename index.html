Give us your Birthday and we will tell you the exact day of the week you were born

>
>

This is a small web-application that takes the user's date of birth and calculates the day of the week on which they were born. It then, depending on the gender of the user, outputs their Akan name. Akan names are Ghanaian names given to children depending on the day of the week they were born.
